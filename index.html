The Open Exoplanet Catalogue

With the recent discovery of the Trappist-1 planetary system and its Earth-sized planets, Exoplanet research has garnered much attention. The following tools allow you to explore the Open Exoplanet Catalogue to find your own Earth-like planet! This collection currently contains over 3000 planets. The data is presented as is but has been initially filtered to a subset which have Surface Temperatures around that of Earth's.

Exploring the data

Use the Parallel Coordinates (PC) chart to refine your selection of planets - draw on each line to select a range; then explore your selection further in the Bar chart by selecting different attributes for the y-axis and get accurate values from the individual tooltips. With these tools see if you can answer the following questions:

  • What basic conditions does Earth have?
  • What are the similarities and differences between Earth and other planets?
